Pixel Prance (Demo)

About: As a former rhythm game enjoyer, I decided it would be pretty fun to create one myself. I created this game to explore more genres to develop and to have some goofy fun with it. I learned in depth about how rhythm games are created and more about score systems inside games. This project was really fun but also very tedious. I understand why rhythm games have their own editors now.

Game Summary: There is no story other than the fact that you are now a colorful pixel wanting to dance. Dance your heart out as you watch the groovy pixel Prance!

How To Play: 
DFJK to tap colors
Tap the coordinated colors when they reach the note boxes
Esc to go to the menu
Try to get your best score and have fun!
